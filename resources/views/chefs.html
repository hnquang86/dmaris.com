About Us

D’Maris is a premier buffet restaurant renowned for its exceptional variety, high-quality dishes, and luxurious dining atmosphere. Originally established in South Korea, D’Maris has become a popular choice for food lovers seeking an elevated buffet experience. The name "D'Maris" translates to "The Sea" in Latin, reflecting the restaurant's commitment to offering fresh seafood as one of its signature highlights.
